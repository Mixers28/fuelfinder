## Description (4000 chars max)

Stop overpaying at the pump.

Fuel Finder uses official UK government data to show you real petrol and diesel prices at stations near you — updated directly by fuel retailers, not guessed or crowdsourced.

**Find cheap fuel instantly**
See every nearby station ranked by price or distance. Spot the cheapest at a glance with colour-coded pins on the map. One tap to get directions.

**Live prices you can trust**
Fuel Finder is built on the GOV.UK Fuel Finder API, introduced under the Motor Fuel Price Open Data Regulations 2025. Retailers are legally required to report their prices — so what you see is what you pay.

**Supports all fuel types**
- E10 (Unleaded)
- E5 (Super Unleaded)
- B7 (Diesel)
- SDV (Premium Diesel)

**Is it worth the detour?**
The Fill Now feature does the maths for you. It calculates whether the saving at the cheapest station is actually worth the extra miles — accounting for the fuel you'd burn getting there.

**Map and list view**
Switch between a scrollable list sorted by price or distance, and a live map with price bubbles pinned to every nearby station. Tap any pin to see the price and get directions.

**Your privacy matters**
No account required. No tracking. No ads. Your location is used only to find nearby stations and is never stored. Full privacy policy at [your website].
