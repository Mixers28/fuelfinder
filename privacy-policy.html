Privacy Policy

Fuel Finder — Last updated 16 June 2026

Fuel Finder is a free app that shows live UK fuel prices near you. This policy explains what data the app uses and why.

What we collect

We collect nothing. No account is required. No personal information is stored by us at any point.

Location

The app requests your location while in use solely to find fuel stations near you. Your coordinates are sent as a query to our server to calculate distances and return nearby results. They are:

  • Never written to a database or log file
  • Never shared with third parties
  • Discarded immediately after the response is sent

You can revoke location access at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → Fuel Finder. The app will not function without it.

Fuel price data

Prices are sourced from the GOV.UK Fuel Finder API, published under the Motor Fuel Price Open Data Regulations 2025. We cache this data on our server to reduce load on the government API. No user data is associated with this cache.

Third-party services

Our server runs on Railway. Only anonymised infrastructure logs (standard HTTP access logs, retained by Railway per their own policy) may be generated. We do not enable any additional logging.

No analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or tracking frameworks are included in the app.

Children

Fuel Finder does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes

If this policy changes materially, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new date at the top.
